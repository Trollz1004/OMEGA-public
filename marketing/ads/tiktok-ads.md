# Spark Dating App - TikTok Ads

---

## Campaign Strategy Overview

### TikTok Ad Objectives
1. **Reach** - Build brand awareness with Gen Z and Millennials
2. **Traffic** - Drive app store visits
3. **App Installs** - Primary conversion goal
4. **Conversions** - In-app signups and premium upgrades

### Key Principles for TikTok
- Native-feeling content performs best
- Hook viewers in first 1-2 seconds
- Vertical format only (9:16)
- Sound-on experience (use trending sounds when appropriate)
- Authentic > Polished
- User-generated content style outperforms branded content

## Targeting Recommendations

### Core Audiences
- Ages 18-34 (primary)
- Ages 25-45 (secondary for conversion)
- Interests: Dating, Relationships, Romance, Love, Self-improvement
- Behaviors: App installers, Dating app users

### Lookalike Audiences
- 1% lookalike of app installers
- 1% lookalike of premium subscribers
- 1% lookalike of engaged users

### Interest Categories
- Lifestyle
- Entertainment
- Relationship content
- Self-care
- Comedy/relatable content

### Exclusions
- Current users (app activity signal)
- Recent installers (7 days)

## Creative Best Practices

### Video Specs
- Format: 9:16 vertical
- Length: 9-15 seconds (In-Feed), up to 60 seconds (allowed)
- Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum
- File: MP4 or MOV, under 500MB

### Performance Tips
1. **Hook in first 1 second** - Grab attention immediately
2. **Sound-on design** - 90%+ watch with sound
3. **Native look** - Polish â‰  performance on TikTok
4. **Text overlays** - Reinforce key messages
5. **Clear CTA** - Tell them what to do
6. **Trend participation** - Use relevant sounds/formats

### Testing Framework
- Test 3-5 creatives per ad group
- Rotate every 1-2 weeks (faster fatigue on TikTok)
- A/B test hooks, sounds, formats
- Monitor comments for sentiment

## Budget Recommendations

### Starting Budgets
- Testing phase: $100-200/day per ad group
- Scaling phase: $500-2000/day for winners
- Always CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization)

### Bidding Strategy
- App installs: Target CPI or Lowest Cost
- Conversions: Target CPA
- Reach/Awareness: Lowest Cost

### Expected Metrics (Benchmarks)
- CTR: 1-3%
- CPI: $2-5
- CPM: $5-15
- Conversion rate (install to signup): 30-50%
