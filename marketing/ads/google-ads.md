## Bidding Strategy Recommendations

### Search Campaigns
- **Brand Keywords:** Target CPA or Maximize Conversions (high intent)
- **Generic Keywords:** Target CPA with gradual budget scaling
- **Competitor Keywords:** Target Impression Share (top of page)

### Display/YouTube
- **Awareness:** Target CPM
- **Consideration:** Maximize Clicks
- **Conversion:** Target CPA

### Recommended Daily Budgets (Starting)
- Brand Search: $500-1,000
- Generic Search: $2,000-5,000
- Display Remarketing: $1,000-2,000
- YouTube: $1,500-3,000
- Performance Max: $3,000-5,000
