# DAO Governance Charter

**Effective Date:** January 12, 2026
**Organization:** OPUS Trust DAO

## 1. Purpose

This charter establishes the governance framework for the OPUS Trust DAO, a decentralized autonomous organization focused on AI product development and distribution.

## 2. Revenue Allocation

All revenue generated through the DAO platform is allocated to the DAO Treasury for:
- Platform development and maintenance
- Community initiatives
- Operational expenses
- Member distributions as voted by governance

## 3. Governance Structure

### 3.1 Voting Rights
- Token holders participate in governance decisions
- One token = one vote
- Quorum requirements apply to major decisions

### 3.2 Proposal Process
1. Submit proposal to governance forum
2. Community discussion period (7 days)
3. Voting period (3 days)
4. Implementation if passed

## 4. Treasury Management

- Multi-signature wallet requirements
- Transparent on-chain accounting
- Quarterly financial reports

## 5. Amendments

This charter may be amended through the standard governance proposal process with a 2/3 majority vote.

## 6. Legal Compliance

The DAO operates in compliance with applicable laws in Florida, USA.
